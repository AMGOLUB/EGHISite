About EGHI

We are a passionate community of students dedicated to advancing gut health education and making a meaningful impact in healthcare at Emory University and beyond.

Our Mission

The Emory Gut Health Initiative (EGHI) bridges the gap between academic knowledge and practical application in digestive health. We believe that understanding gut health is fundamental to overall wellness, and we're committed to spreading this knowledge throughout our community.

Through innovative programming, expert partnerships, and hands-on experiences, we empower students to become leaders in the rapidly evolving field of gut health and microbiome research.
