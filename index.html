Our Mission

The Emory Gut Health Initiative (EGHI) is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting awareness, education, and community engagement about the essential role gut health plays in our overall well-being. Through expert guest lectures, student volunteering, hospital outreach, and interactive workshops—including gut-friendly cooking—we empower future healthcare leaders and the broader community with knowledge and practical tools to prioritize digestive health as the foundation for lifelong wellness.
